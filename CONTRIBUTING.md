# Contributing to VANTROSA

Thank you for your interest in VANTROSA.

VANTROSA is a luxury, privacy-first digital platform developed with a curated and controlled contribution model.  
This repository is not an open-source project in the traditional sense.

## Contribution Policy

At this stage, VANTROSA does not accept unsolicited public contributions.

All contributions are:
- Invitation-only
- Strategically aligned
- Reviewed under internal VANTROSA standards

## Why This Policy Exists

VANTROSA is designed as a premium digital ecosystem.  
To protect its architectural integrity, brand positioning, and security principles, contributions must remain selective and aligned with long-term vision.

## How to Engage

If you are:
- A strategic partner
- A designer
- A developer
- A brand collaborator

And believe you can add value to VANTROSA, you may express interest through official VANTROSA communication channels.
